Who We Are

🌿 Our Mission: We’re building a platform that helps learners visualize their progress like a tree — where each branch is a skill and every leaf is growth.

📚 How It Works: Pick a topic, track your learning, and see your tree evolve. Whether it’s coding, art, language, or science — every step is recorded as part of your journey.

🤖 Powered by AI: Smart paths, goal tracking, and personalized resources designed just for your pace and interests.

🌍 Built for Everyone: Study solo or with friends. Skill Tree is ad-free, open-source, and learner-first.

🚀 The Vision: Democratize knowledge. Empower minds. Grow trees of wisdom together.
